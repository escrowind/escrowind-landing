Why EscrowIND?

The Problem

Online deals on OLX, Facebook, Instagram or WhatsApp are full of risk:

  • Buyers lose money after paying in advance
  • Sellers lose products when buyers refuse to pay

Our Solution

EscrowIND solves this problem. We provide a secure escrow service that protects both buyers and sellers in online transactions.

Trust & Security

Building safer online transaction platform for everyone

How It Works

Simple, secure, and straightforward process that protects everyone

1

Secure Payment

Buyer pays into EscrowIND where we hold money securely until transaction completion.

2

Product Ships

Seller ships the product with tracking information provided to both parties.

3

Buyer Confirms

Buyer receives product and confirms satisfaction through our platform.

4

Payment Released

EscrowIND releases payment to the seller. Transaction complete!
